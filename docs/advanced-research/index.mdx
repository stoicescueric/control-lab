# Module 6 · Advanced Research

The final module goes beyond what most teams implement, into the territory of original research. It is
built around a projectile-trajectory simulation: predicting where a launched game element lands, given a
realistic model of the physics.

## What this module covers

- **Subsystem control (shooting)** — flywheel velocity control and look-up tables mapping distance to
  launch parameters.
- **Numerical integration** — RK4 versus Euler, and why integration order changes your landing point.
- **Quadratic air drag** — modeling drag that scales with the square of velocity, where the closed-form
  parabola breaks down.
- **Trajectory simulation** — assembling the above into a predictor you can run on the robot.
