# Advanced Topics & Misc

These are **optional deep-dives**. Each one extends a core lesson with the heavier mathematics or a
specialized technique that would slow a first read of the main path. Come here once you are already
comfortable with [PID](../control-theory/pid.mdx), [localization](../localization-odometry/odometry.mdx),
and [kinematics](../path-following/mecanum-kinematics.mdx). This section also holds useful side quests
that are too specialized for the main sequence. Nothing in the core curriculum depends on this section,
and every page below names the core lesson it builds on.

## What's here

- **[Guided Vector Fields: convergence theory](./gvf-convergence.mdx)** - the implicit / level-set
  formulation $\chi = E\nabla\varphi - k\varphi\nabla\varphi$ and the Lyapunov proof that the field drives
  a robot onto the path and keeps it there. Extends the intuitive
  [guided-vector-field lesson](../path-following/guided-vector-fields.mdx).
- **[Inverse Kinematics: Jacobians & Numerical Solvers](./inverse-kinematics-jacobians.mdx)** - the
  advanced form of IK: use a Jacobian to translate small end-effector errors into joint-angle corrections,
  then stabilize the solve with damping near singularities. Extends
  [Inverse Kinematics](../path-following/inverse-kinematics.mdx).
- **[How does Pedro Pathing work?](./how-pedro-pathing-works.mdx)** - a research-to-robot bridge from
  Bezier curves and closest-point projection to guided-vector-field correction, centripetal feedforward,
  heading control, and completion constraints. Extends [Guided Vector Fields](../path-following/guided-vector-fields.mdx).
- **[Take-Back-Half](./takeback-half.mdx)** - the one-line upgrade that turns a chattering bang-bang
  flywheel into a steady, ripple-free hold. Extends [Bang-Bang Control](../control-theory/bang-bang.mdx).
- **[Natural cubic splines: the tridiagonal derivation](./natural-cubic-derivation.mdx)** - solving for
  the spline's interior moments with one $O(n)$ tridiagonal sweep (the Thomas algorithm), and why a robot
  path needs two of them. Extends [Splines](../path-following/quintic-splines.mdx).
