🤔 What's the difference between a filter and a controller?

They're the two halves of almost every smart machine — a drone, a self-driving car, a washing machine, your phone's step counter.

🌊

A filter cleans up what you know

Sensors are noisy and shaky. A filter takes messy measurements and estimates the true value — like wiping fog off a window so you can see clearly.

Kalman · EKF · Low-pass
🎮

A controller decides what to do

Once you know where things are, a controller chooses the action — more thrust, less heat, turn left — to reach your goal and hold it there.
